About Me

I am a PhD Researcher in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore, India. I am associated with the Nanoscale Devices, VLSI Circuits and Systems (NSDCS) Lab and currently working as Project Staff under the Chips to Startup (C2S) Program.


My research interests include Edge-AI accelerators, low-power VLSI architectures, FPGA-based AI systems, RISC-V processor design, transprecision computing, and hardware-efficient deep learning accelerators.

Research Interests
